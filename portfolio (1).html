Why a butterfly

The butterfly is more than just a symbol to me; it is a personal representation. It embodies lightness and an apparent fragility, yet it remains elusive and difficult to capture: always moving, always exploring. With its vibrant colors and unpredictable paths, it reflects my deep curiosity and countless interests across art and technology. To me, the butterfly stands for continuous metamorphosis: an enduring drive to transform, adapt, and never stop improving.
